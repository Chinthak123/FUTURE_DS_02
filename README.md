# FUTURE_DS_02
Customer Churn & Retention AnalyticsThis project focuses on identifying the key drivers of customer turnover (churn) for a subscription-based business. By analyzing customer demographics, account information, and usage patterns, we provide actionable insights to improve customer lifetime value and reduce revenue leakage.
📁 Project Structurechurn.ipynb: The primary Jupyter Notebook containing data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and churn driver identification.WA_Fn-UseC_-Telco-Customer-Churn.csv: The dataset containing 7,032 customer records with 21 features.🛠️ Tools UsedPython 3Pandas & NumPy: Data manipulation and cleaning.Matplotlib & Seaborn: Data visualization and trend analysis.
📊 Key Insights & Findings1. Retention HeatmapThe analysis reveals a high concentration of churn within the first 6 months of the customer lifecycle. Once a customer reaches a tenure of 20+ months, the probability of churn drops significantly.2. The "Contract" EffectCustomers on Month-to-Month contracts represent the highest risk segment. Businesses can significantly boost retention by incentivizing transitions to One-year or Two-year contracts.3. Financial DriversMonthly Charges: There is a positive correlation ($0.19$) between higher monthly bills and churn, suggesting price sensitivity.Tenure: Tenure is the strongest negative predictor of churn ($-0.35$), confirming that loyal, long-term customers are much less likely to leave.
💡 Business RecommendationsBased on the data, I advise the following retention strategies:Targeted Early Intervention: Implement a "New Customer Success" program specifically for users in their first 3-6 months to lower the high early-stage churn rate.Contract Optimization: Offer discounted "Loyalty Rates" for customers who switch from month-to-month plans to annual commitments.Senior Citizen Support: Senior citizens showed a higher propensity to churn; specialized support or simplified billing structures for this demographic could improve satisfaction.🚀 How to RunEnsure you have Python installed.Install dependencies:Bashpip install pandas numpy matplotlib seaborn
